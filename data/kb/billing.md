# AcmeInsure Billing & Payments

## Payment Methods
AcmeInsure accepts credit card, debit card, and direct bank debit. Monthly and annual payment plans are available. Annual payment includes a 5% discount versus paying monthly.

## Billing Cycles
Monthly payments are taken on the same calendar day each month as the policy start date. If that day does not exist in a given month (e.g. the 31st), payment is taken on the last day of that month.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails, AcmeInsure retries after 3 days and notifies the customer by email. If payment fails twice, the policy enters a 14-day grace period. Cover continues during the grace period, but a claim made during this time may be affected if payment is not resolved. If unpaid after the grace period, the policy lapses.

## Refunds
Refunds from cancellation within the cooling-off period are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Prorated refunds after cancellation are issued minus the $25 administration fee.

## Discounts
- Multi-policy discount: 10% off when holding both auto and home policies.
- No-claims discount: up to 30%, increasing 5% per claim-free year.
- Annual payment discount: 5%.
Discounts stack but total discount is capped at 40%.
