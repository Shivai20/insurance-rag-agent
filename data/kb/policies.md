# AcmeInsure Policies

## Auto Insurance Coverage Tiers
AcmeInsure offers three auto policy tiers: Basic, Standard, and Premium.
- **Basic**: Third-party liability only. Covers damage you cause to others. Does not cover your own vehicle.
- **Standard**: Liability plus collision and theft. Covers your own vehicle for accidents and theft, with a $500 deductible.
- **Premium**: Everything in Standard plus zero-deductible glass repair, roadside assistance, and a courtesy car for up to 10 days during repairs.

## Home Insurance Coverage Tiers
- **Essential**: Building structure and fixed fittings against fire, storm, and flood.
- **Complete**: Essential plus contents cover up to $50,000 and accidental damage.
- **Complete Plus**: Complete plus high-value item cover (single items up to $10,000) and home emergency cover.

## Policy Changes and Cancellation
Customers may amend their policy at any time through their online account or by contacting support. Changes to coverage take effect at the start of the next billing cycle unless an immediate change is requested. Customers can cancel within a 14-day cooling-off period for a full refund, provided no claim has been made. After the cooling-off period, cancellation incurs a prorated charge for the days covered plus a $25 administration fee.

## Renewal
Policies auto-renew annually unless the customer opts out at least 7 days before the renewal date. Renewal quotes are sent 30 days in advance. Premiums may change at renewal based on claims history and risk reassessment.
