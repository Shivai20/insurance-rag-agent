# AcmeInsure Support & Escalation

## Contact Channels
Support is available via chat (this assistant), phone (8am–8pm local time), and email. Emergency roadside assistance (Premium auto) and home emergency cover (Complete Plus) operate 24/7 via dedicated hotlines.

## When a Case is Escalated to a Human
The assistant escalates to a human agent when: the customer explicitly requests a human; the request involves a complaint or dispute; the request concerns a declined claim appeal; legal or regulatory matters are raised; or the customer is in a vulnerable or distressed situation. Escalations create a ticket and a human agent responds within one business day.

## Complaints
Complaints are logged and acknowledged within 2 business days. AcmeInsure aims to resolve complaints within 8 weeks. If unresolved, customers may refer the matter to the independent Financial Ombudsman Service.

## What This Assistant Cannot Do
This assistant provides information and can look up account details and claim/policy status. It cannot make binding commitments, approve or deny claims, change premiums, or offer financial or legal advice. Those actions require a human agent.
