# AcmeInsure Claims

## How to File a Claim
Claims can be filed online, via the mobile app, or by phone. You will need your policy number, the date and description of the incident, and supporting evidence (photos, police report number for theft or accidents). Once submitted, you receive a claim reference number immediately.

## Claim Processing Timelines
- **Auto glass-only claims**: typically resolved within 2 business days.
- **Standard auto claims**: assessed within 5 business days; payout or repair authorization within 10 business days of assessment.
- **Home contents claims**: assessed within 7 business days. Large or disputed claims may require an in-person assessor visit, adding up to 10 business days.
- **Theft claims**: require a police report reference number before assessment can begin.

## Claim Status
Customers can check claim status anytime in their online account. Statuses are: Submitted, Under Review, Assessor Assigned, Approved, Payment Processing, Closed, or Declined. A declined claim includes a written reason and information on how to appeal within 30 days.

## What Affects a Claim Outcome
A claim may be declined if the incident is not covered by the policy tier, if the policy was lapsed at the time of incident, if there is evidence of misrepresentation, or if required evidence is not provided within 30 days of filing.

## Excess / Deductible
The deductible is the amount the customer pays toward a claim. Standard auto is $500; Premium auto glass is $0. Home deductibles are $250 for Essential and Complete, $0 for Complete Plus home emergency cover.
